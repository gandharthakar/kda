KDA - Kalasagar Digital Arts

Kalasagar Digital Arts. is a locally owned photo business that has operated in the kalyan east area for over 25 years. We are well recommended for local weddings in kalyan region in our area and considered one of the specified, non-corporate studios in the kalyan. Our specialties include wedding photography, portraits, executive sessions, families and so much more. We also work as a videographer and editing of footage's in analogue and high definition coverages.
